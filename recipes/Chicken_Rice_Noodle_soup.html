Easy Chicken, Rice, and Noodle soup

Description

This easy chicken, rice, and noodle soup features fresh mushrooms and rotisserie chicken. With only a few fresh veggies to chop, this is perfect for days when you are short on time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, sliced
  • 6 baby carrots, sliced
  • 4 ounces white mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups shredded cooked rotisserie chicken

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a large stockpot over medium-high heat. Add onion, celery, carrots, and mushrooms. Cook until onions are translucent, about 4 minutes.
  2. Add broth and chicken. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add rice packet, reduce heat, and simmer 8 minutes. Turn heat off and let stand for 8 minutes. Ladle into bowls and serve.
