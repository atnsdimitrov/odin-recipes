Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is baked in the oven to create a warm, hearty meal that is perfect for feeding a crowd. The rich, savory flavor of the meat sauce, combined with the creamy texture of the cheese and the tender pasta, makes it a favorite comfort food worldwide.

This recipe features a traditional lasagna made with ground beef, marinara sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. It is layered and baked to perfection, ensuring every bite is a delicious combination of flavors. Serve it with a side of garlic bread or a simple salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 12-15 sheets, cooked or no-boil)
  • Ground beef (1 lb)
  • Italian sausage (1/2 lb, optional)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz)
  • Mozzarella cheese (2 cups shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup grated)
  • Egg (1, beaten, to mix with ricotta)
  • Marinara sauce (4 cups)
  • Tomato paste (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute. Then, add the marinara sauce and tomato paste. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Then, layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses on top.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
